CustodyGuard

Helping self-represented parents turn a pile of custody paperwork into searchable, court-ready records.

What it does

CustodyGuard is a web application for pro se litigants in family-law proceedings. It organizes custody documents into structured, searchable, court-ready records — reducing the overwhelm of self-representation and helping parents present their case clearly.

My role

As founder and product lead, I own the full product lifecycle: requirements and scoping, UX design, an AI-assisted build (HTML, CSS, JavaScript), testing, git workflow, deployment, and DNS — informed by domain expertise in custody and family law.
