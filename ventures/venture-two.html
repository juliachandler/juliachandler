Coppermap

A family support calendar — mapping the day, together.

What it does

Coppermap is a family support calendar: a simple, visual way to map out the day and the month, with reminders and shared family access so everyone stays coordinated. It's built to reduce the mental load of keeping a household and its schedules on track.

My role

As founder, I lead the product end to end — from concept and UX through build, deployment, and the family-sharing features that keep everyone on the same page.
